Daily Mindfulness

18th March 2017

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

I was a little self conscious about this. Instead of practicing it formally, I have been practicing it without really telling anyone in an effort to become a better listener. I may not be the best listener so I really hope that this does help me to improve. I am going to practice some of the 'looping' techniques to ensure the person I am talking to knows that I am listening properly.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

I am going to work on doing some mindfulness 'training' at least once a day. I have been ensuring I stop and attempt to take follow my breathing while I am having a bad time at work. This has been great at helping me to slow down and actually get back to a calm level and work through things, rather than ending up in a panic.

Any other musings?

The mindful walking sounds like something I want to practice. I walk a lot. I try to get out at least once a day for a walk on the Waterfront. I am going to try and walk 'mindfully' while doing this over the next week to see how I feel.
