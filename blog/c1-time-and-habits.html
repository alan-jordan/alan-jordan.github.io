Time and habits

  • Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?
  • I tried the Pomodoro technique and the time box technique to work through some readings.
  • What did you find interesting?
  • I found the readings and videos on habit making very interesting. I particuarly like the idea of the Fogg method to build habits which will help to make long term changes to behaviour.
  • What worked for you?
